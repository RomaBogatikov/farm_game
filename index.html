Game rules

So, you have decided to try yourself as a farmer. Using the money borrowed from the bank you have already bought a barn in the location you have selected where you can breed different animals. Each year you have to pick animals you want to breed or plants you want to grow (later in the game). You can find the price above the picture of each animal or plant, and the generated yearly profit below. Your goal is to earn more than one million dollars in 10 years to retire early and comfortably.
